Hello my name is Dominic Waters and I just recently returned to the US after speding five years living abroad in Asia. I spent five years living abroad. And while it was never part of my plan I enjoyed every minute of it and I hope that you will have a new found respect for living abroad. As well as develop an interest in Asia after looking at this site. I'm using this blog to help document my life abroad not only for myself but also for anyone else who is interested in going abroad. Or anyone else who is tired of being told what they should do with their future and instead focus on what they want to do with their futures. I'm nowhere near an English major so please forgive any mistakes. Additionally I'm using this site and the blog contained to help practice my coding so please forgive any frequent changes or updates. I hope everyone here will have a better understanding of life abroad and the choices that you can make that will help you enjoy a more developed and interesting life.
